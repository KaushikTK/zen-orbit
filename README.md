# 🪐 Zen Orbit | Evolution

A minimalist, hyper-casual orbital survival game built with high-performance HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript. Navigate the void, collect energy sparks, and survive the relentless onslaught of geometric anomalies.

## 🎮 Live Demo

[Play the game now](https://kaushiktk.github.io/zen-orbit/)

## 🚀 The Concept

Zen Orbit | Evolution is a game of rhythm and reflexes. You control a celestial entity locked in a dual-orbit system. By manipulating your gravitational pull, you must weave between the inner and outer rings to avoid collisions while harvesting energy to sustain your shield.

## ✨ Core Features

One-Button Mechanics: Simple yet deep gameplay—Hold to pull inward, release to drift outward.

Dynamic Difficulty: The game intensity scales as your score increases; enemies spawn faster and move with higher velocity.

Shield System: Collect Yellow Sparks to charge your capacitors. Once at 100%, an energy shield activates, granting you a second chance upon collision.

Kinetic Feedback: Features screen shake, particle explosions, and smooth trail rendering for a satisfying "juicy" feel.

Persistent High Scores: Compete against yourself with local storage persistence that saves your best "Mission" record.

Responsive Design: Fully adaptive Canvas rendering that works seamlessly on Desktop (Mouse) and Mobile (Touch).

## 🛠️ Built With

HTML5 Canvas API: For high-performance 60FPS rendering.

Tailwind CSS: For sleek, modern UI overlays.

JavaScript (ES6+): Clean, modular logic with normalized physics delta-timing.

LocalStorage: For client-side data persistence.

## 🕹️ How to Play

Start: Click the "Start Mission" button.

Navigate:

Click & Hold (or Touch): Pulls your entity into the Inner Orbit.

Release: Drifts your entity back to the Outer Orbit.

Survive: Avoid the Red Square enemies.

Collect: Hit the Yellow Sparks to fill your Shield Bar.

Shield: When your bar is full, you are protected from one hit.
